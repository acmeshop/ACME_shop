Dear participant,
For the next 20 minutes you will play the role of an employee assigned to the online sales of a computer products store.

Your job is to take care of the entire sale and sales process following each of its phases: from the initial inquiry, providing advice and information, and to the fulfillment of the order.

Being a simulation of a real job, try to identify yourself in the role and to perform every task to the best of your ability. For each request you can spend as much time as you need before passing to the next request.
Try to be as accurate and precise as possible in carrying out your role following your own personal pace.
